🎬 Konten Lucu – Versi Realistik + Voice Over

Senin – “Bangun Pagi versi Realita”

💡Ide

Kamu berusaha bangun pagi dengan semangat, tapi setiap kali alarm bunyi, ekspresimu makin lelah. Akhirnya bangun jam 10 dan bilang “masih pagi kok… di London.”

🎥Prompt Video

Realistic video of [photo of you attached], lying in bed with messy hair, eyes half open, irritated by a loud alarm clock. The morning sunlight enters the room softly. The person grabs the phone, turns off the alarm, looks at the camera, and mutters sarcastically, “still early… in London.” Real lighting, cinematic tone, soft humor vibe, realistic skin texture, 4K, vertical format.

🎙️Voice Over

(Sleepy, sarcastic tone)
“Katanya bangun pagi bikin sukses… yaudah, sukses telat aja dulu. Masih pagi kok… di London.”

Selasa – “Ngopi Biar Nggak Emosi”

💡Ide

Kamu duduk lemas di meja kerja, kelihatan bete. Begitu minum kopi, langsung berubah jadi energik, penuh semangat — sampai terlalu semangat.

🎥Prompt Video

Realistic video of [photo of you attached], sitting at a desk looking exhausted, dark under-eye circles, blank stare. Then taking a sip of coffee from a mug. Suddenly, their eyes widen and they smile brightly, typing fast with exaggerated enthusiasm. Warm café lighting, steam from the cup visible, ASMR background of coffee pouring. Vertical format, cinematic realism.

🎙️Voice Over

(Calm to energized tone)
“Sebelum kopi, dunia kayak musuh. Setelah kopi… aku siap menaklukkan tugas, kerjaan, dan drama kantor.”

Kamis – “Tiba-Tiba Dewasa”

💡Ide

Kamu menatap jauh ke jendela, teringat masa kecil yang pengen cepat dewasa. Potongan visual masa kini: tagihan, kerjaan, deadline, wajah pasrah. Ending: “Pengen refund masa kecil.”

🎥Prompt Video

Realistic cinematic video of [photo of you attached], staring out the window with a nostalgic expression. Soft lighting, bokeh city background. Quick cuts of them paying bills, tired at computer, sighing deeply. Subtle voice-over “I used to want to grow up… now I want a refund.” Warm film tone, real human textures, emotional realism.

🎙️Voice Over

(Soft, reflective tone)
“Dulu pengen cepet gede… biar bebas. Sekarang udah gede, malah pengen refund. Ternyata hidup tuh, bukan level up — tapi mode survival.”

🎥Prompt Video

Realistic short video of [photo of you attached], frustrated with a slow smartphone. Their tapping becomes harder, they sigh loudly, roll eyes, then look at the camera with mock anger. Realistic indoor lighting, cinematic detail, expressive acting, subtle humor tone, vertical format.

🎙️Voice Over

Sabtu – “Kucing Saya vs Saya”

💡Ide

Kamu dan kucingmu punya kebiasaan mirip — kamu kerja, dia tidur di keyboard. Kamu makan, dia ikutan ngelirik. Ending-nya: kalian rebahan barek.

🎥Prompt Video

Realistic video of [photo of you attached] sitting at a desk while a fat orange cat lies lazily on the keyboard. The person gently moves the cat, but it refuses. Both end up lying down together on the sofa. Natural daylight, cozy home interior, warm tone, realistic fur and skin texture. Comedic, wholesome vibe.

🎙️Voice Over

(Playful, amused tone)
“Katanya kucing niru pemiliknya. Sekarang dia suka rebahan, makan tiap dua jam, dan ngantuk pas jam kerja. Jadi sebenernya siapa yang niru siapa?”

Durasi: 15–25 detik | Style: cinematic, relatable humor, real acting, vertical (9:16)

Senin – “Autocorrect Si Pengkhianat”

💡Ide

Kamu kirim chat serius, tapi autocorrect malah ubah jadi kalimat memalukan.

🎥Prompt Video

Realistic video of [photo of you attached], sitting at a desk, texting seriously on phone. Suddenly, shocked expression after seeing autocorrect change the word into something embarrassing. (Frustrated, sarcastic tone)
“Autocorrect tuh bukan bantu nulis, tapi bantu ngerusak reputasi. Satu huruf salah, langsung dikira flirting.”
